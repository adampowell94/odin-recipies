Grilled Sandwich

Description

A grilled sandwich is two slices of bread filled with cheese and tomato, before being toasted.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 slices cheese
  • 1 tomato, sliced thinly
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Butter both slices of bread on one side.
  2. Fill buttered bread with cheese, before adding tomato slices.
  3. Put sandwich together, before placing into grill or sandwich press. Butter outside of sandwich to taste.
  4. Cut sandwich into half once cheese has melted and bread is toasted golden brown.
