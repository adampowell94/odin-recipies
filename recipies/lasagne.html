Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is multiple sheets of pasta filled with beef ragu in between each layer, with a red sauce.

Ingredients

Ragu:
  • 200g ground Beef
  • 1 cup plain tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup diced carrot
  • 1/2 cup diced pumpkin
  • 1/3 cup red wine

Pasta:
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 5 sheets pasta

Steps:

  1. Saute√® carrot and pumpkin in pan over med-high heat with ground beef until fat has rendered. Add tomato sauce, mashing meat into small crumbles until it has browned.
  2. Add wine and bring to the boil, reduce heat to low and simmer for 1 hour, stirring occaisionally
  3. Start preparing pasta once sauce has been cooking for 1 1/2 hours. Divide pasta into 4 equal sections.
  4. Spread ragu over base of a square baking dish. Add a layer of pasta to the top. Repeat layers until you get to the top of the dish and finish with cheese on top
  5. Bake in oven at 175 degrees Cel. until sauce is bubbling and cheese has melted on top.
